Falterboy is a Tampa based indie rock Americana project fronted by West Michigan native Jeremy Schantz. Temporarily relocating back to West Michigan where frontman Jeremy was raised, the band released their debut Jammie nominated album Blasphemy there, before returning to Tampa. (check it out here!) The album has been featured on Grand Rapid's WYCE as well as Tampa's WMNF where they have performed live. They have also been featured on the always ground breaking Unsigned with Jill Elinor. But don't take our word for it check out what Sleeping Bag Studios had to say! They are currently working hard on a follow up album.
