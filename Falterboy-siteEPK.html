In the vast landscape of musical artistry, there are those artists who dare to walk the path less traveled, forging a unique and compelling sound that transcends convention. Falterboy, the brainchild of singer-songwriter Jeremy ‘Buff’ Schantz emerged from the humid heart of Tampa, breaking free from the confines of traditional genres and bringing to life a sonic universe that combines the raw power of rock with the introspective depth of indie and the infectious energy of pop punk.
Falterboy took its first breath when Jeremy, a seasoned songwriter and musician, decided to explore the depths of his creative vision. Following his tenure with a number of bands, Jeremy embarked on a solo journey, channeling his songwriting talents and innermost thoughts into a project that would become synonymous with resilience.
Falterboy's music defies easy classification, occupying a niche where alternative rock, punk, and indie sensibilities coalesce. Jeremy's soul-baring lyrics weave tales of personal and universal struggles, inviting listeners on a journey of introspection and emotion. His evocative voice, often tender yet brimming with intensity, is complemented by his skillful guitar work, which paints the musical canvas with vivid strokes of melody and emotion.
Falterboy's discography is a testament to Jeremy's evolution as a musician and storyteller. His debut album, "Blasphemy (2015), showcased his talent for crafting emotionally resonant songs that speak to the human condition. A sophomore release is tentatively scheduled for release early next year.
Jeremy's live performances are magnetic experiences where the audience becomes an integral part of the music. Whether performing in intimate venues or commanding grand stages, his energy and passion invite listeners to immerse themselves in his songs. The connection between artist and audience is palpable, forging an unforgettable experience at each show.
Jeremy's journey as Falterboy is a testament to his unwavering dedication, resilience, and artistic commitment. He has garnered the respect of music enthusiasts and industry insiders alike. His music transcends boundaries, challenges norms, and delivers a message of hope, tenacity, and the enduring power of self-expression. In a world filled with noise, he reminds us that authenticity and resilience are the keys to creating music that truly matters. Jeremy ‘Buff’ Schantz, as Falterboy, stands as a testament to the enduring human spirit, inspiring us all to embrace our unique voices and persevere in the face of adversity.
